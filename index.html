Duluth Minnesota

A vibrant jewel in the crown of Minnesota's North Shore.

Top three attractions of Duluth

Canal Park

The cultural hub of Duluth. The place for dining, entertainment. Be sure to see the Aerial Lift Bridge or participate in Grandma's Marathon.

Enger Tower

Rising above Lake Superior, Enger Tower is known for it's views. From the top, you can see two states along with the beautiful expanse of Lake Superior.

The North Shore

Enjoy a ride on one of the many scenic byways of the North Shore. Over 120 miles of stretching from Duluth to the Canadian border, there is no better way to relax!
